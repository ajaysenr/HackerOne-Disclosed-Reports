## Program Summary

> Our website was not updated to include our new twitter handle, and resulted in pointing to an old one that we disavowed. As a result, the bug bounty participant was able to register that Twitter handle and could have masqueraded as us. Not an actual security vulnerability or exploitation, but a mistake that was worth addressing and rewarding for.
