## Program Summary

> The researcher highlighted the fact that the Google Maps API key (which is by design easily retrievable from the .apk) was missing some restrictions. It then could be used by anyone to query the Google Static Map API, and possibly lead to financial damage. 
Resolved by enforcing missing restrictions.
