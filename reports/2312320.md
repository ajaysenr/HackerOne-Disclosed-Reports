## Program Summary

> @alpernae was able to demonstrate that there was no check for email verification, allowing for a user to authenticate into the [Enjin Platform](https://platform.enjin.io) without ever having verified their email address. This has been addressed and it is now impossible for a session to be initiated without email verification being completed.
