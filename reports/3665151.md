## Summary:
brave shields shows the domain like 1.attacker.com as attacker.com.1 

**steps to reproduced**
1. go to brave browser ( currently i am using ubuntu as os and browser version is 
Brave 1.89.132 (Official Build) (64-bit)
Chromium: 147.0.7727.56
2. go to 1.1.1.1.attacker.com
3. click on brave shield and you can see that the site name changed to attacker.com.1.1.1.1

## Impact

Misrepresentation of domain structure in Brave Shields can mislead users into trusting attacker-controlled sites, enabling phishing attacks.
