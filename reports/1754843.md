## Program Summary

> Please note: Storefront XSS is listed as a Known Issue on our [policy page](https://www.shopify.com/bugbounty/criteria?q=known-issues) and the vast majority of storefront XSS reports received on our program are closed as Not Applicable.

The `return_page_pathname` querystring parameter on the following URL was vulnerable to XSS by using the `javascript:` protocol:

`https://[YOUR-SHOP].myshopify.com/admin/marketing/reports/[MARKETING-CAMPAIGN-ID]?return_page_pathname=%2Fadmin%2Fmarketing&return_page_title=Marketing%20overview`

Attack Complexity was assessed as high, because there are specific conditions for this XSS to successfully execute.

For example:

`https://[YOUR-SHOP].myshopify.com/admin/marketing/reports/[MARKETING-CAMPAIGN-ID]?return_page_pathname=javascript:alert('xss')&return_page_title=Marketing%20overview`

To be impacted, an authenticated staff member would need to:

1. Visit a link with an XSS payload in the link
2. Click the back button before the page is fully loaded.
