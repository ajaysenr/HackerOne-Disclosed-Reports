## Program Summary

> Per our policy page, we accept Staff Member Permission issues that have one of the following impacts:

1. Demonstrates the ability for a staff member to escalate their privileges to one of our Sensitive Permissions (Manage Settings, Manage Payment Settings and Themes).
2. Demonstrates a direct financial impact on the Shop (The request made must directly result in the impact).
3. Demonstrates impact on Buyer PII that the staff member isn't already authorized for.

This report falls under number 1 above as it demonstrated the ability of a staff member to escalate their permissions to Manage Themes permissions by visiting a vulnerable URL.
