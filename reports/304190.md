## Program Summary

> The Heartbleed Bug was a serious vulnerability in the popular OpenSSL cryptographic software library. This weakness allows stealing the information protected, under normal conditions, by the SSL/TLS encryption used to secure the Internet. This allows attackers to eavesdrop on communications, steal data directly from the services and users and to impersonate services and users.
