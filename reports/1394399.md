## Program Summary

> Dear Rocket.Chat Team

While inspecting our logs I noticed, that the OAuth Tokens are leaked in plaintext in the logs. I wanted to draw your attention to this, as this is a security vulnerability. See the attached Screenshot for a redacted log excerpt. 

In my opinion, the best approach here would be to substitute all tokens for a placeholder.
