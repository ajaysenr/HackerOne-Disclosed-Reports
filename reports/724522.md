## Program Summary

> The endpoint used for rating the driver did not validate correctly if the trip corresponded to one made per user logged in, therefore knowing the tripUUID, driverUUID and userId made it possible to change the rating of any trip. Attack scenario: a bad driver with poor ratings and programming skills could change the ratings of their past trips to artificially inflate their rating.
