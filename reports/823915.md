## Program Summary

> This was a DoS based on triggering a lot of bounced emails via SES service which could put our email sending up for review with AWS. The vulnerability was due to unrestricted invitations on sandbox programs which allowed an attacker to generate an infinite number of bounced emails. We had applied a fix by limiting program invites in sandbox programs, but it was an incomplete fix as demonstrated by @iamr000t.

*Why Limited Disclosure?*
We don't usually limit disclosure, but there were a lot of emotional responses and far too much to redact that would have made it difficult to follow the report.
