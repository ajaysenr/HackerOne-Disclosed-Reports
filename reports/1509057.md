## Program Summary

> An Insecure Direct Object Reference (IDOR) vulnerability was found on a TikTok Seller endpoint via the "promotion_id" parameter, which could have resulted in a promotion being remover from a user's account. We thank @aidilarf_2000 for reporting this to our team.
