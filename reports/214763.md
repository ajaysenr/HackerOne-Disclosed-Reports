## Program Summary

> This report involved the injection of control characters, such as Null Byte `0x00`, into the profile Bio field in order to cause unexpected, harmful behaviors. Our solution was to both block control characters from being saved on the backend when included in user-input, as well as to suppress the output and rendering of previously-submitted control characters.
