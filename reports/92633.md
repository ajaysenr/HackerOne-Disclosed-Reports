## Program Summary

> The Shopify Point of Sale application is compatible with a new EMV card reader from [Miura](http://www.miurasystems.com/products). The researcher discovered that the firmware for this device contains a wifi password configured in wpa_supplicant (presumably used during factory tests). However, due to the absence of wifi interface on the Miura devices sold by Shopify, this was not considered to be an issue.
